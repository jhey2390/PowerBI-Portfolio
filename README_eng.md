### 2024 Content Update Project

This report presents the monitoring of the “Territorial Deployment” strategy, implemented by the Peruvian Government during 2024 to update and provide digital educational materials for students and teachers.

Through Power BI, information from the work carried out by technology specialists nationwide was consolidated, allowing users to visualize progress by educational institution, specialist, and goal achievement. The dashboard facilitates results monitoring and provides key information for decision-making in educational management.

### 2025 Perú Compras Analytics Observatory Project

This report allows users to visualize and analyze public procurement carried out nationwide through the Electronic Catalog of Framework Agreements (CAEM) of Peru’s Central Public Procurement Agency.

Using Power BI, purchase orders issued by Peruvian public sector entities were consolidated, enabling the analysis of prices, quantities, types of goods, catalogs, and purchasing patterns. The dashboard contributes to monitoring the commercialization of goods and identifying possible atypical behaviors in public procurement processes through CAEM.

### Project: Induction Sessions by Territorial Agents for Teachers and School Leaders

This report presents the monitoring of induction sessions carried out nationwide to strengthen the capacities of teachers, specialists, and school leaders in the use of digital educational materials.

Through Power BI, information on the activities conducted, participants reached, and additional training needs identified is consolidated, facilitating the monitoring of training actions and supporting decision-making.

### Project: Technological Resources in Educational Institutions Nationwide

This report consolidates information on the technological resources available in educational institutions nationwide, using data from the Educational Census conducted annually by the Ministry of Education of Peru.

Through Power BI, information from the 2018–2022 period is integrated, facilitating the analysis of the availability of technological resources in educational institutions. The dashboard aims to support Ministry specialists in decision-making and in the design of strategies that strengthen teaching practices and student learning.

![Project 04](https://github.com/jhey2390/PowerBI-Portfolio/blob/31da2d0a0bc762b3e2d45941fa0284e29e59c939/proy-04.gif)

### Project: Use of Google Accounts

This report analyzes the use of academic accounts provided by the Peruvian Government, in partnership with Google, during the remote education period in 2022. This initiative enabled more than 5 million accounts for students and teachers nationwide.

Through Power BI, information on account usage and the main Google Workspace for Education applications, such as Google Classroom and Google Meet, is consolidated, facilitating the monitoring of digital tool adoption within the education system.
